Castelli Event Consultants

Our company was founded in 2013 and has over 30 years combined experience with booking exhibitor space for a diverse range of companies at events such as trade shows, conventions, exhibits, expos, sports events and festivals worldwide. We have provided our services to retailers, manufacturers, wholesalers and service based companies alike.

With over 20,000 events each year in the United State alone, it can be challenging to choose the right venues to market your business. We specialize in working with our clients to find, book and execute events that provide the best ROI and “bang for the buck”.

Whether you are a small business looking to test new markets or a large enterprise that needs help maximizing the marketing budget. Our service is perfect for all types of businesses that are looking for a solid presence in the trade show and event industry.

Our formula to booking successful events is unique and second to none. We use a combination of internal databases, market trends, research, connections and our own experience to find our clients the best events combined with our bulk purchasing power and innovative negotiation techniques to save our clients thousands on their marketing budgets.

We strive to establish win-win partnerships with all of our clients, celebrating their successes, while learning from the mistakes all in effort to be a one-stop solution provider for all of our clients needs.
